# Planogram Compliance Checker

Takes image recognition output from shelf audit tools and compares actual product placement against the approved planogram specification — checking facings, product sequence, shelf position, and price label presence. Computes a compliance score per fixture and store, flags out-of-compliance positions with specific deviation details, and generates a corrective action report prioritized by compliance impact and store traffic volume. Supports retail execution teams and field sales organizations tracking planogram adherence across store networks.
