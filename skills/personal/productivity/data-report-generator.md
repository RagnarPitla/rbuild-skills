# Data to Report

You have a spreadsheet. Your boss wants a report. You have 20 minutes.

This skill turns raw data into a professional report with real insights — not just a table reformatted. It finds what matters, surfaces the story in the numbers, and writes it in plain language a non-analyst can understand.

## Works With Any Data

- Sales reports (monthly revenue, by product, by region)
- Survey results (NPS scores, multiple choice responses)
- Website analytics (traffic, conversions, top pages)
- Team performance data (tickets closed, hours logged)
- Customer data (sign-ups, churn, LTV by segment)
- Financial data (P&L, budget vs actual, cash flow)
- Any CSV or spreadsheet you can paste

## How to Use

**Step 1:** Paste your data (CSV, copy from Excel, or just describe it)

**Step 2:** Tell Claude what you need:
```
"Create an executive summary report from this sales data. 
Audience: VP of Sales. Focus on: regional performance and 
product mix. Highlight any anomalies."
```

**Step 3:** Get your report

## What the Report Includes

**Executive Summary** (3-4 sentences — the whole story up front)

**Key Metrics** (the numbers that matter most, bolded)

**Top Insights** (what's surprising, what changed, what's working)

**Trends** (direction of travel — improving, declining, flat)

**Anomalies** (what stands out, what needs investigation)

**Recommendations** (what to do next, based on the data)

**Data Notes** (any caveats, gaps, or assumptions)

## Report Formats

Tell Claude which format you need:
- **Executive summary** — 1 page, prose, for leadership
- **Operations report** — detailed, with tables, for your team
- **Slide bullets** — formatted for copy-paste into PowerPoint
- **Email format** — ready to send directly
- **Dashboard narrative** — context paragraph for a data dashboard

## Trigger Phrases

- "Turn this data into a report"
- "Create an executive summary from this spreadsheet"
- "What are the key insights from this data?"
- "Analyze this and write a report for [audience]"
- "Data report — [paste data]"
- "Make sense of these numbers"

## Example Prompt

```
Here's our Q1 sales data by region. Create an executive summary 
for our quarterly business review. Highlight the top 3 insights, 
flag any regions below target, and recommend where to focus in Q2.

[paste your data]
```

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Report too generic | No audience specified | Add "Audience: [role], Focus on: [topic]" |
| Missing context | Claude doesn't know your targets | Add "Our target was X. Previous period was Y." |
| Too long | Default format | Ask for "executive summary only, under 200 words" |
