# Image Prompt Generator — Example

## Scenario: Featured Image for a LinkedIn Article on Agentic ERP

**Who:** Ragnar Pitla, writing a LinkedIn article titled "The ERP System of 2027 Runs on Agents"
**Where:** LinkedIn article featured image, displayed at 1200x627px, needs to work as a standalone visual even without the headline text
**The Problem:** Ragnar needs a featured image that communicates "AI agents operating inside an enterprise ERP system" without defaulting to the cliche visuals that dominate the space: glowing robots, generic circuit boards, or stock photos of people staring at dashboards. The image needs to feel architectural and professional, match his brand (clean, technical, forward-thinking), and be generatable via Lovart.ai or Gemini Nano Banana.

**Prerequisites:** The article topic and the image generation tool you want to target. Lovart.ai for stylized illustration, Gemini Nano Banana for photorealistic or semi-realistic renders.

---

### What You Say to the Agent

> "Generate an image prompt for a LinkedIn article featured image. Topic: agentic ERP — AI agents as first-class participants inside an ERP system. I want it to feel architectural, not sci-fi. No robots, no glowing brains. Think: clean 3D isometric illustration showing interconnected process flows (procurement, finance, supply chain) with small agent icons operating autonomously at key nodes. Color palette: deep navy, electric blue, clean white. Target tool: Lovart.ai."

### What the Agent Does

Produces a complete, ready-to-paste image prompt:

```
Lovart.ai prompt:

Isometric 3D illustration of a modern enterprise system architecture.
Clean, minimal style. Deep navy background (#0A0F2C). Three interconnected
process towers representing Procurement, Finance, and Supply Chain, each
rendered as translucent glass blocks with glowing circuit-line interiors
in electric blue (#2E8BFF). Small geometric agent icons (hexagonal, white,
softly glowing) positioned at decision nodes within each tower, connected
by thin animated-style light trails suggesting real-time data flow.

No humans. No robots. No dark dramatic lighting. Corporate architecture
feel — like a high-end consulting firm's AI strategy deck visual.
Top-down isometric camera angle. 16:9 ratio, 1200x627px target.
White logo safe zone in bottom-right corner.
```

Also generates a Gemini Nano Banana variant for a photorealistic style if requested, and an alt-text string for accessibility.

### The Business Impact

- **First image passes the "scroll test"** — stops a LinkedIn user mid-scroll because it does not look like every other AI article image
- **Brand consistency** — same visual language across Ragnar's LinkedIn articles, YouTube thumbnails, and newsletter headers
- **No design tool required** — prompt goes straight into Lovart.ai or Nano Banana, image is ready in under 90 seconds
