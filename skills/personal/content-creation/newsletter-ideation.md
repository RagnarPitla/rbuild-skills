# Newsletter Angle Generator

You know the topic. The problem is you're about to write the same thing three other people published this week.

This skill generates non-obvious angles on any topic — the contrarian take, the underexplored angle, the story nobody told yet.

## How to Use

```
Generate newsletter angles for the topic: [your topic]
My audience: [who reads your newsletter]
What I want to avoid: [the obvious take / what everyone else is saying]
```

## The Angle Frameworks

**Contrarian angle** — challenge the consensus. "Everyone says AI will replace writers. They're wrong — here's what's actually being replaced."

**Jobs-to-be-Done** — what is the reader actually trying to accomplish? "You don't want to 'learn AI' — you want to spend less time on stuff that drains you."

**SCAMPER** — Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to other uses, Eliminate, Reverse. Systematically finds new angles by modifying the conventional approach.

**The underground angle** — what do practitioners know that's not public yet?

**The historical angle** — "This happened before. In 1995, when the internet came, [parallel situation]. Here's what that predicts."

**The systems angle** — zoom out. What larger system does this topic sit inside?

**The personal story angle** — ground the abstract in a specific, real experience.

## Output Format

```
TOPIC: [Your topic]

ANGLE 1 — Contrarian
Headline: [Working title]
The take: [2-3 sentences on the angle]
Why it works: [What makes this fresh]

ANGLE 2 — Jobs-to-be-Done
[Same format...]

[5-7 total angles]

RECOMMENDED ANGLE
[Which one to write and why, based on your audience]
```

## Trigger Phrases

- "Generate newsletter angles for [topic]"
- "What's a fresh angle on [topic]?"
- "I'm writing about [topic] — what's not obvious?"
- "Newsletter ideation — [topic]"
- "5 angles on [topic]"

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Cause | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Still feels generic | No audience specified | Add "My audience is [specific type of person]" |
| Angles too similar | Topic too broad | Narrow the topic first: "not AI in general, specifically AI for solo creators" |
| Can't pick one | All angles sound good | Ask: "Rank these by engagement potential for [audience]" |
